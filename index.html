Who we are, and what we do!

Zelda Global is a non governmental organization that focuses on mentoring models on a right path to make it big in the fashion industry. We are all about capturing art and spreading talent worldwide. with our collaborations with Bollywood and Tollywood celebrities and mentors, we provide support to our members, mentor and guide them into the fashion industry.
